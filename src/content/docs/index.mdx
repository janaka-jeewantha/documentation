## From SDLC to AI-DLC

The SDLC is becoming the **AI-DLC**: an AI-Driven Lifecycle where agents in a coordinated mesh collaborate seamlessly, so your engineers can focus on work that only humans can do. GitHub & Azure DevOps · Claude-powered · Docker-ready · Open Source.

Retrofitting AI into an unchanged delivery process often creates more noise than value. Better results come from shorter feedback loops, clearer handoffs, and explicit human accountability. Xianix connects AI directly to delivery tools, keeps feedback tight, and places people at the decision points that matter.

## The Agent Team

Five specialized agents support the delivery flow, each with a clear trigger, a defined output, a simple definition of done, and an explicit human checkpoint. Shared artifacts — not tight runtime coupling — are what make this a team: backlog context flows into design, design and implementation flow into review, review feeds testing, and approved change signals feed release preparation.

## Human judgment as the control point

In AI-DLC, people do not disappear from the process — their role becomes clearer: set direction, add context, and decide when AI output is good enough to move forward.

| AI suggests | Human decides |
|---|---|
| Detailed work plan — breaks an intent into tasks and sub-tasks | Adds context, removes work that doesn't support the goal |
| Architectural options — sync vs. async, storage choices, service boundaries | Reviews key decisions based on risk, cost, and team knowledge |
| Step-by-step directions — implementation steps, test ideas, next actions | Keeps work on course and corrects direction when needed |
